# Reflection: Reputation-Based Verification in Bounties

Reputation systems create long-term incentives for honest participation without requiring economic deposits. This is more inclusive and encourages consistent good behavior.

**Public vs Hidden:** Commitments and reputation scores are public. Answers stay hidden until reveal. Reputation changes are transparent.

**AI vs Human:** AI provides objective scoring. Participants vote with reputation-weighted power. This combines AI efficiency with human judgment.

**Advantages:** No economic barrier to entry, encourages long-term participation, transparent reputation system.

**Trade-off:** Requires ongoing reputation management and may be slower to build trust initially.
